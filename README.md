# Veyra Pilot Intelligence

**Aviation assessment and training analytics platform**

Veyra Pilot Intelligence is a digital platform designed to support aviation training through structured assessment, academic follow-up, and performance-based analytics.

The platform begins with a Pre-Solo evaluation module and is designed to support future training and assessment workflows under CBTA and EBT principles.

## Purpose

Veyra helps students, instructors, and aviation schools transform assessment results into useful information for:

- Academic feedback
- Identification of knowledge gaps
- Follow-up of student performance
- Detection of critical areas for reinforcement
- Training decisions based on evidence

## Current version

The current pilot version includes:

- Assessment selection interface
- Pre-Solo evaluation module
- Student data registration
- Timed examination
- Result storage through Supabase
- Internal dashboard
- Individual attempt review
- Group and individual performance insights
- CSV export for academic follow-up

## Training approach

Veyra integrates principles of:

- CBTA — Competency-Based Training and Assessment
- EBT — Evidence-Based Training
- KSA100 — Knowledge, Skills and Attitudes approach

The goal is to support more objective, traceable and useful academic evaluation in aviation training.

## Pilot implementation

This version is being used as an academic pilot project to evaluate its usefulness, clarity, traceability, and potential for supporting real training environments.
